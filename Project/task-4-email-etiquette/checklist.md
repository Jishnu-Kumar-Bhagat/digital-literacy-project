### Responsible Social Media Use Checklist



### **Do’s**



* ##### Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
* ##### Think before posting—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
* ##### Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
* ##### Maintain a positive digital footprint (professional and clean profile).
* ##### Respect others’ privacy—ask permission before posting photos or tagging.



### Don’ts



* ##### Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, passwords).
* ##### Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or negative comments.
* ##### Don’t post offensive, inappropriate, or illegal content.
* ##### Don’t overshare details about your daily life or location in real-time.
* ##### Don’t fall for suspicious links, scams, or fake accounts.
